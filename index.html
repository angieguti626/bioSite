Monica Gutierrez is a University graduate who has had many accomplishments including participating in the National Exchange Program to study in New York, completing a Disney College Program Internship in Florida, working the Super Bowl Halftime Shows for two years, being a Box Office Coordinator and more.


Some achievements include being on the Dean's list every term while she was in school and doing numerous extracurricular activities in school such as Speech & Debate, Black History Bee, and Drama Club. She also has done activities outside of school such as cheerleading, baton twirling, and soccer. She has also help manage numerous theatre shows every Spring.

Monica has many goals she would like to achieve. She hopes in the future to move to New York and work in Broadway. As well as also trying to work for the Disney Cruise Line and travel around the world. She loves The Walt Disney Company and would love to work with them. She has many aspirations and is trying to do them all!


She has traveled to numerous places including New Orleans to volunteer while representing her University. Monica has also traveled to Ensenada on a school trip to study. Her favorite place has been New York due to it's city life and all the theatre history the city holds. The most recent place she has traveled to is Las Vegas due to the Super Bowl. She hopes to be able to continue her work in the upcoming year as well.
